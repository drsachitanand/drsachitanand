## 👨‍⚕️ About

Dr. Sachit Anand is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Paediatric Surgery at the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi. His academic interests span minimally invasive paediatric surgery, paediatric urology, evidence synthesis, and systematic reviews.
